Our Story

A legacy of excellence and leadership

Gyan Jyoti Pathshala, established in 2051 B.S., has been a trusted center of academic excellence and character building. With more than 700+ students, we proudly nurture responsible, confident, and skilled individuals for the future.

Our institution focuses not only on academic success but also on leadership, discipline, creativity, and moral values. We believe every child has unique potential and we are committed to helping them shine.
